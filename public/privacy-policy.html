Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 30, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how the Nigeria Customs Service (NCS) Computer Based Test (CBT) System collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Your National Identification Number (NIN), name, and contact details.
  • Biometric Data: Facial recognition data captured during the verification process.
  • Test Data: Your test responses, scores, and performance metrics.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Verify your identity and authenticate your access to the test
  • Administer and monitor the examination process
  • Evaluate your test performance and generate results
  • Improve our services and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Secure data storage and retention policies

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
